Taroko National Park





The number one attraction in Hualien is definitely Taroko National Park. It is famous for its majestic and almost vertical marble canyon landscape. It is a must-see attraction for foreign tourists visiting Taiwan. As long as you follow the canyon scenery of Liwu River, you can easily see cliffs and other scenery, which is breathtaking. In Hualien Taroko National Park, waterfalls are an important landscape!

Hualien County





Hualien is the largest county in Taiwan. It faces the Pacific Ocean to the east and the Central Mountain Range to the west. With its majestic mountains, blue sky, vast ocean, and beautiful longitudinal valleys, it has become the first choice of Taiwanese people's favorite tourist county and city, and is deeply loved by the people of Taiwan.
